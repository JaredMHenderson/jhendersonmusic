    ABOUT JARED


    Jared Henderson is a drummer based out of Colorado Springs CO. Jared was fortunate to grow up in the rich history of New Life Church and the Desperation movement. Years of playing live and recording with Desperation Band and New Life Worship have developed a passion for excellence and given Jared experience touring the world and recording live/studio projects.

    With a reputation for consistency, hard work, and reliability, Jared has also toured and/or recorded with Michael Rossback, Paul Baloche, Kari Jobe, Cory Asbury, Michael Farren, Jared Anderson, Meredith Andrews, Alissa Turner, Gateway, All About Worship Collective, and Travis Ryan as well many other independent artists.

    Jared offers live/remote drum recording sessions, drum programming, and demo production. Click here for rates and booking information.
